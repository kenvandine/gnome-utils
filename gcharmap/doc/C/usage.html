Program Usage


1. How to select characters

You can select characters by clicking at the button that has the same character.
If your mouse entered the button, you will see the character in the black box (the Previewer). Now the choosed character will be added to the entry box.
You are not required to click at buttons; you can also type with your keyboard.
Click at the entry box and type it.
When you're done, click at the Copy button to copy the text to clipboard.

2. Select more characters

If you can't find the character you want in the buttons, then you can use this feature:
Click at the Edit menu and choose "Intert ASCII Character..."
Now you will see a dialog.
Every ASCII character has a number, range from 0 - 255.
Enter the value. When you changed the value, you will see a preview of that character.
If you found the character you want, click at the OK button to insert the character and close the dialog.
Click at the Apply button to insert the character, but not close the dialog.

Cut, Copy and Paste

You see the Cut, Copy and Paste buttons in the toolbar or at the right side of the main window.
Here's a description of the buttons:
  • Cut: The same as Copy, but also delete the text
  • Copy: Copy the text to clipboard
  • Paste: Paste the contents of the clipboard to the entry box
